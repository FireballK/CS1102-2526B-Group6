Real world example

Formerly Nutmeg

J.P. Morgan Personal Investing

Key Features

  • Fully Managed portfolios with Smart Alpha
  • Active adjustments to ETFs for growth
  • Risk levels: 1–10 (Conservative to Aggressive)
  • Low minimums: £100 for Lifetime & Junior ISAs
  • Tax-efficient wrappers (ISAs, Pensions, etc.)
Fees: 0.35% – 0.75% (depending on portfolio style and balance)
Real-World Performance:
Risk Level 6: +96.4% cumulative since inception (~5.1% annualized). Outperformed is significantly.

Best For: Hands-off UK investors who want J.P. Morgan’s expertise with some active management and a strong tax wrappers.

Low-Cost Leader

Vanguard Digital Advisor

Key Features

  • Pure low-cost index ETF portfolios
  • Highly diversified global exposure
  • Digital (fully automated) or Personal Advisor (hybrid)
  • Excellent for retirement & long-term investing
Fees: Digital: ~0.15% – 0.20% net
Personal Advisor: ~0.30% net
Real-World Performance:
Strong market-tracking performance. 3 years annualized returns at around 14% in recent bull markets. Focuses on consistency over outperformance.

Best For: Cost-conscious, long-term investors who aims low fees and simplicity.
